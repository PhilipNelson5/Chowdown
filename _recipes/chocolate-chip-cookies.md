ingredients:
- ⅓  Cup shortening
- ⅓  Cup butter
- ½ Cup sugar
- ½ Cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 Teaspoon vanilla
- 1 ½ Cups flour
- ½ Teaspoon baking soda
- ½ Teaspoon salt
- ½ Bag chocolate chips
- ½ Cup nuts (optional)

directions:
- Preheat oven to 375
- Mix fats and sugars
- Add in Egg, vanilla and dry ingredients
- Mix in chocolate chips
- Bake for 8-10 minutes

---

Makes 2 dozen
