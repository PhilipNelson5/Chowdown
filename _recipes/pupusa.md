title: Pupusa

tags: 
recipeYield: 8

ingredients:
- 2 cups masa harina
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1/2 tsp salt

directions:
- mix salt and masa harina
- add water and knead until combined
- let the dough hydrate for 10 - 15 minutes
- divide into balls
- stuff with filling
- brush with oil and cook till browned
