tags: dessert

recipeYield: 2 dozen

ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups creamy peanut butter
- 1 stick butter ( softened )
- 1 tbs vanilla
- 2 ½ cups powdered sugar
- 1 bag of chocolate chips

directions:
- Mix ingredients together
- Form into balls
- Refrigerate
- Dip in chocolate and refrigerate
